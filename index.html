Stream the unexpected!

SegnoSharp is a simple audio streaming tool that can randomly stream audio files to Shoutcast/Icecast servers. Enjoy an engaging and unpredictable listening experience every time you hit play.

Get started

  • Smart database

    Effortlessly manage your audio library for smooth streaming and quick track access.

  • Dynamic Playlists

    Automatic, rule-based playlists for engaging, curated streams tailored to your preferences.

  • Extendable Design

    Customize and expand SegnoSharp to fit your unique needs with easy-to-extend architecture.

Licensing

SegnoSharp is licensed through the MIT License. This means it's free of charge and does not have any licensing costs. Basically, you can do whatever you want as long as you include the original copyright and license notice in any copy of the software/source. Read the full license here

SegnoSharp uses the BASS libraries from un4seen developments. You may need to register for a license. See their website, and the SegnoSharp FAQ for more information.

Support

In case you encounter issues with SegnoSharp you can use GitHub's issue tracker to see if your issue is a known issue (with possible workarounds), or you can create a new issue. Please be verbose when creating new issues to help with troubleshooting and debugging.

You can also use the discussion forums and ask the community.
